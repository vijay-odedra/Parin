Shelf Life Information

Best Before Three Months From Manufacture

To ensure optimal taste and quality, we recommend consuming Khari Soda within three months from the date of manufacture. The manufacturing date is clearly printed on each bottle.

For best results, store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, refrigerate and consume within 2-3 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Khari Soda made of?

Khari Soda is made from rice flakes, sugar, cinnamon, citric acid, preservatives, natural coconut oil, sodium benzoate, BHT (BHA), and other additives. All ingredients are food-grade and approved for consumption.

How long does Khari Soda last?

Khari Soda has a shelf life of three months from the date of manufacture. The "Best Before" date is clearly printed on each bottle. Once opened, we recommend refrigerating and consuming within 2-3 days.

Where is Khari Soda manufactured?

Khari Soda is manufactured by Jay Ramnath Balaji Park at Plot No. 19, At: Tramba, Kasubaghar, Rajkot - 360020 (Gulbarga).

Is Khari Soda suitable for vegetarians?

Yes, Khari Soda contains no animal-derived ingredients and is suitable for vegetarians.
